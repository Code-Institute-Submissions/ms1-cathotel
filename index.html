    About

    Animals have always been a part of our lives, and we had an opportunity to make animals a part of our working life.

    So why a cattery? Well it fitted in with our property and surroundings, plus the fact that boarding cats is not well catered for in South Wiltshire, so the plan was launched to build our very own Cattery.

    We have invested in the highest quality chalets, made by Britain's leading UPVC Cattery manufacturer Pedigree Pens They are double glazed, centrally heated, well lit, spacious, and set in a wonderful garden which is always full of birds and butterflies, oh and our resident flock of chickens. So there is plenty of visual stimulation for our guests to view as they survey their surroundings and enjoy the Wiltshire sunshine.
